ABOUT US



At Quick Calligraphy, we are passionate about sharing the timeless art of calligraphy with learners worldwide.

Our mission is to provide exceptional calligraphy education in three distinct styles: Western, Arabic, and Oriental. With experienced instructors and flexible online programs, we make it easy for you to learn and grow in this beautiful art form, no matter where you are.

At Quick Calligraphy, we believe that calligraphy is more than just beautiful writing. it's a form of self-expression and a gateway to understanding diverse cultures.

Our courses are designed to cater to all skill levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

MS. RABIA FAREED

FOUNDER | CEO

Ms. Rabia Fareed is the dynamic founder of Quick Calligraphy, an innovative online institute dedicated to the timeless art of calligraphy.

Her journey began with a deep passion for this elegant craft, and she envisioned creating a platform where enthusiasts from around the world could explore and master calligraphy from the comfort of their own homes. Driven by a commitment to making calligraphy accessible and relevant in the digital age, Ms. Fareed established Quick Calligraphy to offer a diverse range of online courses tailored to all skill levels.

Under her guidance, Quick Calligraphy has become a leading destination for calligraphy education, blending expertise with a user-friendly approach. Through her work, Ms. Fareed continues to inspire and nurture a love for this exquisite art form, ensuring its enduring beauty and cultural significance.

WESTERN CALLIGRAPHY

Explore the elegant curves and flourishes of Western calligraphy, with its rich history and varied styles, including Gothic, Italic, and Copperplate. Our instructors guide you through each stroke and technique, helping you create stunning pieces that reflect your personal style.

ARABIC CALLIGRAPHY

Delve into the intricate beauty of Arabic script, where tradition meets artistry. Learn the nuances of Diwani, Naskh, and Thuluth scripts, and discover how to blend form and function in your writing. Our courses highlight the cultural significance and artistic heritage behind each script.

ORIENTAL CALLIGRAPHY

Experience the grace of Oriental calligraphy, including Chinese and Japanese styles. From the fluid brushstrokes of Chinese calligraphy to the expressive characters of Japanese Kanji, our programs offer a deep dive into these ancient practices, enabling you to master techniques that have been honed over centuries.

OUR PROGRAM

With our flexible online programs, you can study at your own pace, access a wealth of resources, and receive personalized feedback from our expert instructors. Whether you’re looking to enhance your skills, start a new hobby, or pursue calligraphy professionally, Quick Calligraphy is here to support your journey every step of the way.
