ORIGINS OF CALLIGRAPHY

Calligraphy traces its roots to the ancient civilizations of Egypt and Mesopotamia, where writing evolved into a form of artistic expression. Each society developed unique writing systems that reflected their culture and values.

In Egypt, hieroglyphics served as an early example of calligraphy, with intricate symbols and designs carefully carved into stone. These hieroglyphs were not merely a means of communication but also an embodiment of beauty and significance...
